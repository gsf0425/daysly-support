❓ 常见问题

  • 如何添加桌面小组件？
    长按主屏幕空白处 → 点击左上角"+" → 搜索"Daysly" → 选择喜欢的样式和尺寸添加。支持小、中、大三种尺寸及锁屏小组件。
  • 语音创建支持哪些格式？
    支持丰富的自然语言，例如：
    · "明天下午3点开会"
    · "10天后出差"
    · "下个月5号生日 每年重复"
    · "3个月后15号交报告"
    自动识别日期、时间、重复频率，并根据关键词选择图标。
  • 如何开启 iCloud 同步？
    前往设置页面，点击"iCloud 同步"即可。请确保设备已登录 iCloud 账号且 iCloud Drive 已开启。
  • 时区功能如何使用？
    创建事件时默认使用手机系统时区。如果事件发生在其他时区（如国际航班），可以手动选择目标时区，倒计时会自动按该时区正确计算。
  • 数据存储在哪里？
    所有数据默认存储在您的设备本地。开启 iCloud 同步后，数据会同步至您的 iCloud 账户。我们不会收集或访问您的任何数据。
  • 如何自动清除过期事件？
    前往设置页面 → "自动清除过期事件" → 选择保留时长（1/3/6/12个月）。超出时限的已过期事件会在下次打开应用时自动清除。

❓ FAQ

  • How to add a home screen widget?
    Long press on the home screen → Tap "+" in the top left → Search "Daysly" → Choose your preferred size and style. Supports small, medium, large, and Lock Screen widgets.
  • What voice formats are supported?
    Rich natural language is supported, such as:
    · "meeting tomorrow at 3 PM"
    · "trip in 10 days"
    · "birthday next month 5th repeat yearly"
    · "report in 3 months on the 15th"
    Dates, times, repeat frequency, and icons are automatically recognized.
  • How to enable iCloud sync?
    Go to Settings and tap "iCloud Sync". Ensure your device is signed into iCloud with iCloud Drive enabled.
  • How does the timezone feature work?
    Events default to your phone's system timezone. For events in different timezones (e.g., international flights), you can manually select the target timezone — the countdown will calculate correctly based on that timezone.
  • Where is my data stored?
    All data is stored locally on your device by default. With iCloud sync enabled, data syncs to your personal iCloud account. We never collect or access any of your data.
  • How to auto-clean expired events?
    Go to Settings → "Auto-clean Expired Events" → Select retention period (1/3/6/12 months). Expired events beyond the limit are automatically removed when you open the app.
